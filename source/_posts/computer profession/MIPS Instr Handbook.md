## MIPS Instructions Quick Handbook
### Arithmetic
```mips
# === caluculate ===
add $rd,$rs,$rt
sub $rd,$rs,$rt
addi $rt,$rs,imm. 
# addu/addiu
mult $a, $b
div $a, $b
mflo $rt
mfhi $rt
# multu/divu

# === Shift ===
sll $rd,$rs,$rt # shift left
srl $rd,$rs,$rt # shift right


```
